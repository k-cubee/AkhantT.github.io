Unique Plants

There are many extraoridnary plants, that never fail to impress us. Here are 10 of these wonderful plants:

Bleeding Hearts

For flowers that look gorgeous, this name is very absurd. Bleeding hearts look like an actual heart that is bleeding, but only with the blooding being white. Asian bleeding hearts can grow up to 120 cm in length and 45 cm in width. It is extremely valued in gardens all over the world and also by florist, who have these flowers sold with great price.

Scientific Name: Lamprocapnos spectabilis

Location: Temperate Woodlands of eastern Asia and North America

Significant Feature(s): A pink heart structure with a droplet beneath

Corpse Flower

Corpse Flower. Another weird name for a plant, is it not? Although this flower does not seem horrible or menacing, it is actually its smell that attacks you. These flowers get their name from their smell of rotting corpses and is characterized by a carrion flower. The corpse plant bears fruits or berries that are orange in color.

Scientific Name: Amorphophallus titanum

Location: Endemic to Sumatra

Significant Feature(s): Long shoot that comes out of the petals; The unbearable odor of the flower
