Unique Plants

Black Bat Flower

The black bat flower is an exotic looking, unusual plant that is somewhat difficult to grow but is rewarding for its unusual shape, texture, and color. These flowers can grow up to 12 inches in width and have long 'whiskers' that seem to be protruding out up to 28 inches. These plants were thought to be insect-pollinated, but studies have shown that they are self-pollinated. The bat flower requires warm temperatures but prefers a shady location. Plant it where it receives indirect light, preferably in a setting with additional tropical understory plants.

Scientific Name: Tacca chantrieri

Location: Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia

Significant Feature(s): Bat-wings like structure of the black petals; Long 'whiskers' protruding out


Dragon Blood Tree

Dragon blood tree is a unique tree that is one of the most peculiar trees in the world. The tree has a distinctive external shape that makes it look like a huge umbrella, as the leaves grow only at the end of branches and point upwards. When cut, red liquid oozes out and this is how the plant got its name. Its fruits are small fleshy berries containing between 1 and 4 seeds. As they develop they turn from green to black, and then become orange when ripe.

Scientific Name: Dracaena cinnabari

Location: Native to Socotra

Significant Feature(s): Red resin that oozes out; umbrella-like structure
