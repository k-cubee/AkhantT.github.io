Unique Plants

African Starfish Flowers

This flower is shaped like a star and has pieces of fur on its petals. The petals themselves are velvety and the color and size may vary according to the species. African starfish flowers give off a foul smell and that is why, it is known as a "carrion plant". A carrion plant produces a smell that attracts flies due to a lack of other pollinating insects.

Scientific Name: Stapelia grandiflora

Location: Native to South Africa

Significant Feature(s): Starfish-like structure; foul smell






Living Stones

Lithops or living stones are a type of succulent plant that are native to southern Africa. The name was derived from the ancient Greek word, litho, meaning stone and ops, meaning face. These plants have evolved to look like rocks to prevent from being eaten. Each lithops ('lithops' is used for both singular and plural) has a pair or more of bulbous leaves. The leaves are covered with a translucent layer that allows light to enter for photosynthesis. Lithops are popular choices for house plants.

Scientific Name: Lithops

Location: Native to South Africa

Significant Feature(s): The whole plant resembles a rock and is hardly ever recognized as a plant
