Flora Park - Welcome To The Wonderful World of Plants!


Plants are all around us; from those smaller than your finger-nails and to those big enough to sleep in. These living things are of great benefit to us as they provide wood, shelter (some indigenous people live on trees), absorb carbon dioxide and produce oxygen, and provide fruits than are nutritionous. However, not all plants look like plants. Some have amazing patterns and designs while others create a stinky smell that drive people away. Here, we'll be talking about some of the most unique plants that can be found in the world. Let's start!
